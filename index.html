How Petrair operates

Trading, banking and inspection under one controlled process.

Transactions are treated as documented physical operations, not casual enquiries. Product origin, counterparty status, inspection, insurance and banking steps are checked before commercial execution progresses.

01Counterparty qualification

KYC, AML and sanctions screening before sensitive transaction details are exchanged.

02Documented terms

Indicative terms are clarified by specification, quantity, delivery window, Incoterm and settlement route.

03Independent inspection

Recognised inspection parties support quality, quantity and transfer documentation where applicable.

04Controlled settlement

Bank-controlled documentation and LC-backed structures are used for eligible transactions.
