Our offering

Our trading products and services

Petrair SA is engaged in the international trading of crude oil, refined petroleum products and selected energy commodities. The company focuses on identifying, structuring and executing physical commodity transactions with established counterparties across global markets.

Activities are guided by disciplined commercial evaluation, prudent risk management and a strong emphasis on transaction execution and counterparty reliability. By combining market expertise, commercial agility and professional operating standards, Petrair aims to build long-term trading relationships and develop a scalable platform capable of supporting repeat transaction flow across multiple products and jurisdictions.

The company's objective is to establish a credible and sustainable presence within international energy markets through consistent commercial performance, operational discipline and responsible growth.

How we operate

Disciplined trading, banking and inspection

Petrair SA operates a structured back-to-back trading model designed to minimise market exposure while maintaining disciplined transaction execution. Transactions are settled under LC-backed mechanisms with pricing linked to Platts benchmarks and independent SGS inspection at every transfer point.

01Inspection

SGS and internationally recognised inspectors at every transfer point.

02Logistics

Third-party shipping and logistics support across corridors.

03Settlement

Bank-controlled, fully documented LC execution.

04Pricing

Platts-linked benchmarks, transparent and auditable.

Counterparty assurance

Official channels and mandate verification

Energy trading attracts false mandate claims and unauthorised approaches. Petrair SA makes its official contact route obvious and asks counterparties to verify any unexpected communication before sharing documents or banking information.
